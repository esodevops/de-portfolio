UK Jobs Data Pipeline

A maintainable batch pipeline that collects UK data-engineering vacancies, cleans and enriches every listing, and delivers analysis-ready data to CSV and PostgreSQL.

Project overview

Replace manual job tracking with a dependable data flow.

Spreadsheet collection becomes stale quickly and introduces inconsistent fields. TechConnect needed a simple workflow its team could rerun, inspect, and extend without a complex platform.

The challenge

Scattered listings, inconsistent data, limited analysis.

Relevant vacancies live across paginated search results and change continuously. Dates, locations, salary descriptions, and title conventions need normalization. Unstructured spreadsheets make reliable reporting and trend discovery difficult.

ETL workflow

From live search pages to structured job intelligence.

The notebook follows pagination, protects against failed requests, transforms raw fields, and publishes the same trusted dataset to two destinations.

01

Request

Requests fetches "data engineer" result pages from the UK Find a Job service.

02

Parse

Beautiful Soup reads each job card and follows the next-page link safely.

03

Transform

pandas cleans dates, locations, missing salaries, and column names.

04

Enrich

Title keywords classify every role by inferred experience level.

05

Load

Clean records are exported to CSV and inserted into a constrained PostgreSQL table.
